Garbage Disposal

Go to Recycling & Waste Minimisation Section to view recycling options and guides available.
We encourage all residents to read the recycling & waste minimisation section before disposing of any items.

Garbage Bin Locations (Red Lid)

Motto 1– Small wrapped up household items can be disposed off in Chutes on each level. Do not dispose of any type of boxes including Pizza Boxes, etc or any large items. Anyone caught dumping large items in the chute will be charged the fee to have the chute unblocked. Larger items should be taken to the main garbage room and placed in a red lid waste or recycling bin.

Motto 2– Garbage Bins located in the Garbage & Recycling Room to the right of the lift in the basement car Park

Motto 3– Garbage Bins located in the Garbage & Recycling Room to the left of the lift in the basement car Park

Motto 4– Garbage Bins located in the Garbage & Recycling room located in the basement car park near the stairs leading to the courtyard. Can also be accessed from private garage and walking to the right on the drive (near the bicycle rack))

Red Lid Garbage Bins

NOT ALLOWED – Paints and solvents, batteries, soil and building materials should never be put in your general waste (or recycling) bins. Sharps (needles) also require safe disposal – call 02 9265 9333 for more details or go to

Disposing of Hazardous Household Waste

Any leftover chemicals you have at home – including paints, solvents, pesticides and even cleaning products should not be put in your household bins. These might be hazardous and should be disposed of safely. Collection Days and Bins
