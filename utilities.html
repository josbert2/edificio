Utilities

Contact your preferred energy supplier to arrange for connection and contact

the Building Manager to get your meter numbers and or readings.

M1 & M2

– Gas Meters are located in the corridor service cupboards or in the basement garage. Electricity meters are located in the electrical cupboard next to the lift on your floor and require a master key from Building Management.

NOTE: In a few instances some meters may be located on adjacent floors.

M3

– Gas meters are located under the kitchen sink and laundry sink inside your unit. Electricity meters are located in the services cupboard next to the lift on your floor. Energy Australia, Ausgrid and other providers all have an Abloy master key to access the meters in the electrical cupboard.

M4

– Gas meters are located in your private garage and under your laundry sink inside. Your electricity meters are located in the electrical cupboard next to the bin room in the basement.


Services technicians have an Abloy Key and an NMB key to access Gas, Electricity and Communications rooms in all residential complexes in Sydney. Alternatively they need to contact Building Management.
