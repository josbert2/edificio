About Motto Apartments

Completed in 2006, Motto was designed by award winning architects Allen Jack & Cottier who received an award for their design from the Urban Development Institute of Australia (UDIA), NSW Landcom Awards for Excellence. Motto is conveniently located 15 minutes drive from the CBD and a short walk to ErskinevilleTrain station. This website serves as a source for all current and prospective occupants to obtain building related information and news as well as improving communication between residents.
